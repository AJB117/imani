Imani Unidad

Our Mission

The primary purpose of this charitable, not-for-profit organization is to infuse social justice into social health by providing Prevention, Education and Advocacy through Community Efforts.

Wisdom corner

Our Vision

In achieving this purpose, the organization will include: African American community members, individuals living with HIV, those challenged by substance abuse, and formerly incarcerated persons, in decision-making, education, advocacy, service delivery, public relations, research and leadership. The organization will provide neighborhood representatives to facilitate workable solutions through community partnerships; develop innovative programs with multiple strategies; and engage in other activities that promote the overall goals of the organization.
